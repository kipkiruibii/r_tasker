CREATE REMOTASKS ACCOUNTS (INDIA)

Job Description

Remotasks is a platform that connects companies and individuals looking for help with tasks that can be completed online with a workforce of remote workers. The tasks typically involve data labeling, image annotation, and other types of annotation or categorization tasks that require human intelligence. Companies often use Remotasks to outsource tasks that are challenging for computers but relatively straightforward for humans. This can include training machine learning models, improving image recognition algorithms, and other similar projects.

In a globalized digital landscape, certain developers may encounter constraints based on their geographical locations, hindering their ability to participate in platforms like Remotask. This task involves the creation of Remotask accounts, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility for developers around the world.

You are required to help create these accounts for the developers outside your country. If you are interested please continue reading the additional information below.
