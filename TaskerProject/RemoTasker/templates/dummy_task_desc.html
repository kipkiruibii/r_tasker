CREATION OF AIRTM ACCOUNTS

Job Description

Airtm is a platform that provides a way for people to access financial services and transfer money across borders. It aims to connect users with a network of tellers who facilitate currency exchanges, transfers, and other financial transactions.

Due to limited geographical privileges, creation of the accounts is not possible in some countries. As a result some developers are willing to purchase such accounts from you. We are the intermediary and foresee this process.

In this task, you are required to help create these accounts for the users outside your country. If you are interested please continue reading the additional information below.

Requirements

Creation of AIRTM accounts requires the following to be met:

  1. Must be an INDIAN CITIZEN
  2. Must have a National Identity Card
  3. Must have an Indian Phone number
  4. Skill Level: None
  5. Age: Over 18 years

How to create Airtm account

Ensure the VPN or Proxy is off

  1. Go to www.airtm.com in your browser
  2. Go to Join Now at the top -right corner of your screen
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions
  4. Ensure your account has been verified

Pay

$10 Per Account
